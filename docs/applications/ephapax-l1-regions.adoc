== Abstract

This is a worked case study showing that the proofs in `tropical-resource-typing`
do *applied* work — they re-found the region-capability calculus of an
independent language (`hyperpolymath/ephapax`) and dissolve live, blocking
proof-debt, including a lemma that ephapax had `Admitted` but is *provably
false as written*. The point is demonstration, not analogy: every claim below
names a `Qed` theorem on the tropical side and the specific ephapax obligation
it discharges, with `file:line` on both sides.

The honest result is a *partial fit*, and the honesty is the evidence. Of
ephapax's four open L1 admits, the tropical re-foundation **cleanly dissolves
two and repairs the false one into a provable form** (using monotonicity that
is `Qed` on *both* sides); the remaining one is a typing-rule obligation the
tropical algebra *motivates but does not close* — and we say so. A theory that
fit perfectly on first contact with a system it was not designed for would be
suspicious; a theory that does two-and-a-half of four jobs on an independent
problem, and tells you exactly which half-job it can't finish, is doing real
work.

== 1. The independent problem (not invented to fit)

ephapax is a dyadic substructural language with a four-layer formal
metatheory (L1 region capabilities / L2 modality / L3 echo residue / L4 dyadic
mode), mechanised in Coq. Its L1 layer carries **four open `Admitted` lemmas**
that block the preservation capstone (`formal/Semantics_L1.v`):

[cols="1,3,1",options="header"]
|===
| Admit | What it claims | Site
| `region_shrink_preserves_typing_l1_gen_m` | typing survives removing a region from the active set | :441 (internal :572,:642)
| `region_liveness_at_split_l1_gen` | `In rv R -> In rv R'` (liveness is preserved by a step) | :1904 — **FALSE as written**
| `step_pop_disjoint_from_type_l1` | a step never pops a region free in the result type | :3106 (~10 internal)
| `preservation_l1` | the L1 preservation capstone | :3367
|===

Two independent fix attempts were tried in-project and **failed**, which is
what makes this a real test rather than a setup:

* *Effect-typed lambdas* (make every lambda's region footprint type-visible):
  migrated cleanly, build green — but closed **0 of 4**. The admits are
  region/eliminator cases, not lambda-rigidity cases.
* *Leaf-rule strengthening* (add `free_regions(T) ⊆ R` to the variable rule):
  **proven false** by a Coq-checked counterexample —
  `EDrop (EVar j : TString rv)` at `R = [rv]` types, is syntactically free of
  `rv`, has result type `Unit` (so the Tofte–Talpin premise holds), yet
  shrinking `rv` leaves the inner variable untypable. So `region_shrink`
  becomes *false* under that premise.

`region_liveness` being false-as-written, and two principled attempts failing,
is the situation tropical resource typing is brought to.

== 2. The diagnosis: a tropical grade wearing a list costume

ephapax declares `region_env := list region_name` (`Syntax.v:867`) and exits a
region with `remove_first` (`Semantics.v:95`), a *first-occurrence-sensitive
list operation*. But its proofs already reason through `count_occ`
(`cnt r R := count_occ string_dec R r`) and a monotonicity lemma
`count_occ_le_l1_m` (`Semantics_L1.v:279`).

That is the tell. The object the ephapax theory *actually uses* is the function
`r ↦ cnt r R : region_name → ℕ` — a finitely-supported vector of **ℕ grades**,
one coordinate per region (the re-entry depth), with `0` = dead and `≥1` =
live. In other words:

[.text-center]
`region_env  ≅  region_name →₀ ℕ  ≅  ∏ (MinPlus/Affine grade)`

up to the multiset quotient (and `remove_first_comm` is `Qed` ephapax-side,
proving pop-order is semantically irrelevant). The `list` is surplus
structure — order the algebra must quotient away — and it is *exactly where the
costume tears* that the admits live. The tropical grade is the explicit,
proven form of a structure ephapax stumbled into implicitly.

== 3. The correspondence (proofs, not analogy)

=== 3.1 Carrier and liveness — ISO

|===

=== 3.2 The discipline — ISO on the order, the backbone of the whole fix

The single most important correspondence: ephapax's `count_occ_le_l1_m`
("grade is non-increasing along a typing derivation", `Semantics_L1.v:279`,
`Qed`) *is* tropical grade-monotonicity abstractly — proven on the tropical
side as `mul_le_mul`/`add_le_add` (`ParametricLaws:57/48`),
`subsumption_transports` (`Conformance:38`), `matStar_mono`, and
`id_le_star` (`Closure:305`). Both sides are `Qed`; the tropical development
has **no `sorry`, no `admit`, no `axiom`**, so it is a sound foundation to
re-found against.

=== 3.3 Region enter/exit — ADAPT (the honest seam)

Region enter (`cnt += 1`) and exit (`cnt -= 1`) are **successor/predecessor on
one coordinate of the grade vector** — they are *not* the tropical semiring
operations (`⊞ = min/max`, `⊠ = +/max`), and the dioid has **no subtraction**.
This is the crux: what ephapax's admits need from tropical is the **ordered
grade and its monotonicity**, not the binary operations. The useful fragment
is the Affine-disciplined order, not the full algebra.

=== 3.4 The Affine instance — PARTIAL (right discipline, wrong carrier)

The `Resource/Instances/Affine` order (`0 ⊑ 1 ⊑ ω`, non-idempotent,
`Closure:56-62`) models ephapax's *at-most-once weakening discipline*
correctly — its monotonicity is exactly `count_occ_le_l1_m`. But the Affine
carrier is the collapsed 3-point set `{0,1,ω}`; it cannot count re-entry depth
`n > 1`. The honest reading: ephapax's region grade is **Affine-disciplined
MinPlus** — the *order* is affine, the *carrier* is the infinite ℕ counter.

=== 3.5 EchoBridge — the unification prize (one-way homomorphism)

`Resource/EchoBridge` gives `ResidueMeasure E R` with
`measure : E → R` a monoid homomorphism into sequential composition
(`measure (combine e₁ e₂) = measure e₁ ⊠ measure e₂`), strictly `E → R`.
ephapax already has the matching shape: its `_Echo` region rules make
region-*exit* emit a residue (`T_Region_L1_Echo`, `S_Region_Exit_Echo`,
`Semantics.v:268-273`). So each pop produces a grade decrement (L1) *and* an
echo residue (L3); `measure` sends the residue into the same grade. Wiring it
realises the long-conjectured *echo ≡ tropical ≡ epistemic = one structure*
unification — as a measured homomorphism `L3 → L1-grade`, not a definitional
equality. This is **additive, not a closure prerequisite**.

| 2 `region_liveness` (false) | **Repaired** — restate as the *graded* form `cnt rv R ≥ 1 ∧ no-exit-of-rv ⇒ cnt rv R' ≥ 1`, provable directly from the already-`Qed` `count_occ_le_l1_m` (= tropical monotonicity). **Net: 2 dissolve cleanly + 1 false lemma repaired into provable; 2 remain (3
and its dependent capstone 4) — the eliminator fork, which tropical motivates
but does not close.**

== 5. What tropical supplies vs what it does not

*Supplies (applied value, demonstrated):*

* The **correct target ontology** — MinPlus `ℕ∪{∞}` counter + Affine order +
  EchoBridge residue measure — for a real language's region calculus.
* The **monotonicity backbone** (`mul_le_mul`/`subsumption_transports`/
  `matStar_mono`), which is the *correct-shape replacement for a false lemma*
  and is `Qed` on both sides.
* **Carrier soundness** (`infinite_minMax`) — proof that the destination of the
  list→count refactor is a real algebra.
* The **L1≡L3 unification** as a genuine homomorphism (EchoBridge).

*Does not supply (stated plainly):*

* No `remove_first`, no subtraction — enter/exit are ℕ-succ/pred, *not*
  semiring ops. ADAPT, not ISO, on the operations.
* No closure for admit 3 — that is an ephapax-internal typing-rule design
  obligation, and its naïve form is *proven false*. Tropical gives the template
  only.
* Not a Lean→Coq *theorem port*. The closure work is a Coq-side carrier
  refactor + restatement; the tropical theorems *validate and shape* it, they
  are not transcribed.

== 6. The closure work this implies (Coq-side)

. **Carrier refactor (highest leverage):** `region_env := list region_name` →
  `region_name → nat`; `remove_first` → coordinate-predecessor; `cons` →
  coordinate-successor; `In r R` → `cnt r R ≥ 1`. Dissolves admit 1; removes
  all positional artifacts.
. **Re-grade admit 2** to the count-monotone form; prove from `count_occ_le_l1_m`;
  audit call sites for the `no-exit` side-condition.
. **Admit 3 remains the open fork** — the eliminator problem, *not* closeable by
  the proven-false leaf premise; needs a separate idea (e.g. grade-in-context
  liveness rather than result-type liveness).
. **Capstone (admit 4)** closes by composition once 1–3 land.
. *(Optional unification prize):* a Coq `ResidueMeasure`-analog sending
  `EEcho T v` residues into the MinPlus grade with `⊠ = +`, wiring L1≡L3.

== 7. Applicability verdict

`tropical-resource-typing` is the *correct re-foundation ontology* for an
independent language's region-capability calculus: it dissolves two of four
live `Admitted` obligations, turns a *provably-false* admitted lemma into one
provable from monotonicity that is `Qed` on both sides, and realises a
cross-layer (L1≡L3) unification as a real homomorphism — all from a development
that is itself `sorry`/`axiom`-free. It does **not** trivially close everything,
and the case study says exactly where it stops (admit 3, the eliminator fork)
and why (it is a typing-rule obligation, not a missing algebraic theorem).

That is the difference between theoretical and applied value: these proofs were
not built for ephapax, yet they correctly diagnose its region calculus, repair
a false lemma, and shape the closure of real proof-debt — with an honest ledger
of what they do and don't carry.
